"The Smell of Markers and Solana's Rise"

That day, Petrov exhaled the phrase: "$HA! will fly." No one believed him. He covered himself with markers, stood on a desk, and shouted "I am a candle!"

At that moment, Solana started to rise. The teachers wrote it off as a coincidence. But we knew — he was connected to the VHS-god.

"The PE Teacher and Tokenization of Vaulting Horse Jumps"

When the school introduced NFT-jumps, the PE teacher began minting every successful jump. Student Lyokha became a whale in one day because he did a somersault. Now he doesn't go to school, he just writes "$HA!" in Telegram and everyone understands.

"Blockchain Test"

The math test was stored in a smart contract. Those who failed were sent to gas fee limbo. One excellent student accidentally bricked the contract — now he's the admin of the entire school. His name is Vitya. He no longer talks.

"Who Erased the Yeralash VHS?"

Someone erased all episodes of Yeralash in the school media library and replaced them with stock charts. Students think it was the vice-principal. He now walks around in a bull costume whispering "Bull trend..."

"The Floppy Disk That Contained $HA!"

One day, due to a power outage, the computer science teacher pulled out an old floppy disk. There was a $HA! smart contract on it. He said: "This is where it all began." Everyone sat in silence. Even the projector started smoking.
